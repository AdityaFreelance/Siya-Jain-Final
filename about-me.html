Say Hello

Hello there, I’m Siya Jain !

Aspiring UX designer based in Atlanta, passionate about crafting experiences that bring smiles and make a difference.

This is a little about me!

Born in India, Raised in Singapore

I was born in Mumbai, India, where I spent my early years surrounded by my parents and grandparents. At the age of seven, I moved to Singapore, where I quickly immersed myself in both academics and extracurriculars. I was an active member of clubs like Model United Nations and HERstory, a student-led initiative focused on female empowerment. I also played on the netball team, balancing sports with my love for the arts. In my free time, you'd often find me painting, dancing, or simply enjoying time with friends.

2014-2023

The Beginning of My Passion for the Arts

My creative journey began at age 5, when I joined one of Mumbai’s most renowned dance schools. From there, I explored it all, from roller and ice skating to a brief (but memorable) stint in state level gymnastics. After moving to Singapore, I immersed myself in visual arts through formal classes, eventually showcasing my work in two exhibitions, becoming the youngest artist in one of them. Long before I discovered UX, it was my love for the arts that shaped how I see, feel, and create.

2010-2023

Discovering UX

As I looked for ways to push my creativity further, I realized that while visual arts helped me express myself, I wanted to create something more meaningful. Something that could impact others and their communities. That’s when I discovered UX. I was drawn to the field’s potential to blend empathy, problem-solving, and design, allowing me to build on my visual arts foundation while creating real-world impact.

2021-2023

Moving to SCAD

I decided to follow my passion and pursue a UX Design major at SCAD, turning a dream into reality. Since then, I’ve immersed myself in every aspect of the field. Joining design events, collaborating with industry leaders like Porsche and Lowe’s, and becoming an active member of the FLUX community.

2023-present

And this is just the beginning...

As I continue to learn, grow, and create, I’d love for you to follow along and be part of the journey.
